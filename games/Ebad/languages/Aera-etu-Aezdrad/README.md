## Introduction
_Aera-etu-Aezdrad_ (ahra-eh-tu-ahz-drahd) _lit: Air of the Mouth_,
or _Aezdradara_ (ahz-dra-da-ra) is the spoken and written language of
the  _Endae-etu-Waera_, or _People of the Sea_, know regionally as
the _Endaewaera_.
